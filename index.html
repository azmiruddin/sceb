SCEB is a platform for financial institutions to take advantage of the blockchain based smart-contracts as templates in everyday banking.

Technology Stack

  1. Ethereum Blockchain
  2. Embark Framework
  3. Solidity - smart-contract language
  4. IPFS - decentralised file storage

Potential benefits

  1. Cost Reductions
  2. Efficiency Improvements
  3. Reduce Risk
  4. Reusable contracts
  5. Less paperwork
